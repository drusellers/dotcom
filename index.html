Be Epic

.Net on Linux

The rise of Software as a Service has put pressures on the traditional licensing model that most companies are used to. Companies are starting to look out side the Microsoft ecosystem for tools that can keep costs low, while enableing the company's ability to turn hardware on and off at will. Old regime licensing models will cause new pains. Instead of continuing to use Windows as the runtime environment, more companies are looking to the successful Mono project to allow them to maintain their investment in .Net but run it on the open *nix platform instead.
